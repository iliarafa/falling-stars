PRIVACY POLICY

Data We Collect

Falling Stars collects minimal data. The only information stored is:

  • Wish text you voluntarily submit
  • Star count from your game session
  • Timestamp of submission

No Personal Information

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, device identifier, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information. Wishes are submitted anonymously and cannot be traced back to any individual user.

How Data Is Used

Submitted wishes are displayed anonymously on the Global Wishlist, aggregated by theme. They are not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Third-Party Services

Wish data is stored using Supabase, a cloud database provider. No analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools are integrated into the app.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the app does not distinguish between age groups.

Data Retention

Wishes are stored indefinitely to maintain the Global Wishlist. Since wishes are anonymous, deletion requests for specific entries cannot be verified or processed.

Changes to This Policy

Updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
