FAQ

How do I play?

Touch the screen to move your character and catch falling stars. Don't let too many slip past.

What is space mode?

After catching enough stars, you launch into space. Move freely in all directions and dodge planets, comets, and debris.

What happens to my wish?

Your wish is submitted anonymously and added to the Global Wishlist. It is grouped by theme alongside wishes from other players.

Is my data private?

Yes. No personal information is collected. Wishes are completely anonymous. See our Privacy Policy for details.

There is no sound.

Tap the SOUND ON button at the bottom of the screen. Your device must not be in silent mode.
